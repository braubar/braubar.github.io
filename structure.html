Structure¶

Generally braubar is divided into three parts:

  • Brewdaemon
  • Socketserver
  • Webserver

Brewdaemon¶

The Brewdaemon manages the whole application and starts the Socketserver and the Webserver as subprocesses. It keeps track of the current state.
